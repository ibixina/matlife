FUTURE DEVELOPMENT ONCE WRESTLING MODE IS COMPLETED:
Booker Mode:
* **Promotion Start Options**:
  - Start with a small independent promotion (local territory)
  - Accept an offer from an established promotion (mid-sized to major)
  - Create a custom promotion from scratch
* **Talent Management**:
  - Sign wrestlers from around the world (indie scene, free agency, international promotions)
  - Contract negotiations with detailed terms:
    - Length, pay, downside guarantees
    - Creative control clauses
    - Merchandise royalties
    - TV appearance guarantees
    - No-cut clauses
  - Talent scouting system for unsigned wrestlers
  - Performance reviews and morale management
* **Show & Event Booking**:
  - Weekly TV shows, monthly PLE events (Pay-Per-View/Live Events)
  - Special event types (tournaments, supershows, international tours)
  - Match card creation:
    - Match types (singles, tag, ladder, TLC, cage, submissions, battle royales)
    - Stipulation selection (No DQ, Falls Count Anywhere, Iron Man, etc.)
    - Match order optimization
    - Time allocation for matches and segments
  - Segment planning:
    - Promo segments (solo, interview, confrontation, contract signing)
    - Backstage segments
    - In-ring segments
    - Vignettes and hype packages
* **Storyline & Feud System**:
  - Dynamic storyline creation with branching paths
  - Feud management with heat tracking (how invested the audience is)
  - Match outcomes that affect feud progression
  - Heel/Face turns and character development arcs
  - Tag team and faction management
  - Manager and valet assignments
  - Interpromotional angles and cross-promotional events
* **Business & Development**:
  - TV and streaming deals negotiations
  - Venue selection and ticket pricing
  - Merchandise development and sales
  - Sponsorship deals
  - Training facility management
  - Brand reputation and promotion image
  - Expansion opportunities (national/international growth)
  - Financial management (revenue, expenses, profit/loss)
  - Bankruptcy risk and recovery options
* **Audience & Media**:
  - TV ratings and demo breakdowns
  - Live event attendance
  - PPV buyrates and streaming viewership
  - Social media buzz and fan engagement metrics
  - Dirt sheet and newsletter coverage
  - Review scores from wrestling journalists
  - Fan surveys and feedback
* **Booking Philosophy & Decision Making**:
  - Different booking styles (traditional, sports entertainment, shoot-style, lucha)
  - Player freedom in match outcomes and story direction
  - Consequences for poor booking decisions
  - Booking pressure from management/ownership
  - Worker safety considerations
  - Wellness policy implementation
* **Roster Development**:
  - Wrestler training and development system
  - Gimmick changes and character rebranding
  - Injury management and recovery tracking
  - Retirement planning
  - Hall of Fame induction process
* **Match Quality & Analysis**:
  - Match ratings based on workrate, psychology, storytelling
  - Crowd reaction scores (heat, pop, silence)
  - Match flow and pacing feedback
  - Wrestler chemistry metrics
  - Post-match reviews and ratings from critics
* **Backstage Politics & Relationships**:
  - Locker room morale and dynamics
  - Wrestler complaints and demands
  - Talent conflicts and backstage heat
  - Booking committee input (optional advisory system)
  - Worker-management relations
  - Unionization and collective bargaining
* **Notable Features for Player Freedom**:
  - Sandbox mode (unlimited money, all talent available)
  - Historical scenarios (book classic eras)
  - Custom scenario creation
  - Community-created content support
  - Detailed statistics and analytics
  - Season mode with saveable careers
  - Match simulation options (quick, detailed, text-based)
  - Booking log and history tracking
  - "What-if" scenarios and alternate timelines
